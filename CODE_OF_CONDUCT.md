# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive environment for everyone.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and considerate
- Giving and receiving feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what’s best for the community and product

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or discrimination of any kind
- Trolling, insults, or personal attacks
- Publishing others’ private information without explicit permission

## Enforcement

To report unacceptable behavior, please open a security-adjacent issue if appropriate or contact the maintainers via the method described in `SECURITY.md`.

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.
